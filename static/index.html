How it work ?
Our software uses a flaw discovered in a wallet by generating unique private keys. This reduces the number of private keys from 2^256 to 2^64 this which means they can be mined easily (although it may take a little time). If you have a minimum of luck, you can earn around 50€ per day but it can climb very easily in the 1000-5000€ per week without having to move a finger by undoing your computer run with the software.

Once the wallet is found, all btc will be transferred to your wallet that you will have entered in the software settings. To each wallet found by the software, a fee of 0.00004 btc (€1.50) is automatically deducted from the wallets found for wallets below €250 and 0.0005 (€18) beyond. These costs will be deducted from you only if you have chosen a monthly license.

Why sell the software? Let's be clear, our goal is to make money. We want to create a private community bringing together dozens of computers to create active income for our users as well as passive income for our team by applying a fee to each wallet containing money found

Is it safe? Yes, the money that the user receives comes directly from our “cold rooms”. The money you uncover is transformed into WBTC (ERC20) then it goes through rocketcash.eth.org.mx and then back in our “cold rooms”

Is this legal? Yes everything we do is 100% legal. You are just finding old wallets inactive abandoned

Do we need a good computer to run it? The software has been optimized well enough to be light and consume very few resources on a computer

Can we open several instances of the software? Yes ! And it's even recommended if you want to maximize your chances of finding one or more wallets

Why do all addresses start with 1? The first generation of Bitcoin addresses begin all with a 1. This means that the software will look for old lost wallets.
